About Us

Located in the capital, naturally our start in the wedding business would be geared towards local brides. RedPillar prides itself in offering its customers a wide selection of wedding favors, gifts and accessories that are sure to compliment their style wedding day. Our attentive staff works efficiently to process all orders in a timely fashion to ensure all of our customer’s weddings go off without a hitch!

Our products are made with the finest materials and are beautifully crafted from all of today’s leading wedding manufacturers. We know first hand how expensive a wedding can be so we work with our manufacturers to offer our products at the lowest selling point allowed by our vendors. All of our products are first quality items direct from the manufacturer. (We do not sell irregular items or liquidated items.). We provide 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. Browse our site to view our large selection of products and discover why people chose RedPillar for all their wedding accessories needs. Have comments or ideas or perhaps just want to chat about all things wedding! Give us a call.
RedPillar is your final destination for sourcing the latest designs in laces, tapes, trims, fabrics, fashion accessories, patches and butti. We cater to nearly every segment of the textile industry, where any form of narrow fabrics or design accessories are used. Industries that we serve include manufacturers of saris, suits, wedding dresses, and all types of party wear and ethnic wear for ladies, gents and children. We are also the one-stop-shop for the Wedding Accessories industry for designer borders, laces, groom turbans, wedding garlands and other related wedding items.

We not only cater the Indian domestic market but also export Indian Wedding items from head to heal and traditional handicrafts for all kinds of Hindu, Muslim and Punjabi Weddings. To spread authentic and unique handmade products globally, we have a large spread network. Further, we assure our buyers for finest quality of all products.
